# Advanced Mapping

The MUME client features a powerful, automated mapping system.

## 📍 Automatic Tracking
As you move in the game, the mapper uses GMCP data to update your position in real-time. Unexplored rooms will be revealed as you enter them.

## 🔍 Navigation
- **Pan**: Click and drag on the map.
- **Zoom**: Use the mouse wheel or pinch-to-zoom on mobile.
- **Z-Axis**: Change vertical layers using the `CTRL + Scroll` shortcut or the on-screen Z-axis indicator.

## 🏗️ Design Mode
Enable "Design Mode" in the mapper toolbar to manually edit the map:
- **Add Room**: Right-click (or long-tap) on empty space.
- **Link Rooms**: Drag from one room to another to create a connection.
- **Edit Details**: Click on a room to change its name, description, or terrain type.

## 📥 Importing Maps
The client supports `.mm2` files from MMapper. 
1. Open the mapper dropdown.
2. Select "Import .mm2".
3. Choose your map file to load your existing world data.
